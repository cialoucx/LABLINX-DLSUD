Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about LabLinx.

What if my registration is still pending?

New registrations require admin approval. You'll receive a confirmation email within 1-2 business days. Check your Outlook junk folder.

Where do I find email confirmations?

Check your Outlook junk email folder — automated messages from LabLinx sometimes get filtered there.

Can I use LabLinx on my phone?

Yes! LabLinx is fully responsive and works on any device with a web browser — desktop, tablet, or smartphone.

How do I borrow equipment?

Once logged in, browse the inventory, select the items you need, and submit a borrow request. You'll be notified when it's approved.
