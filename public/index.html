Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about using LabLinx.

Use your DLSU-D Microsoft account (@dlsud.edu.ph or @hs.dlsud.edu.ph) to sign in. No separate registration is needed — just click "Log In with Microsoft" on the login page.
LabLinx is available to all DLSU-D students, faculty, and lab administrators. Students can browse and request equipment, while admins can manage inventory and approve requests.
After logging in, go to "Lab Equipment" on your dashboard, find the item you need, and click "Request." Fill in the quantity and reason, then wait for admin approval. Once approved, you can pick up the equipment from the lab.
Overdue items are flagged in your dashboard and admins are notified. You'll receive email reminders before and after the due date. Consistent late returns may affect future borrowing privileges.
Yes! Use the Reservations feature to book equipment ahead of time. You can set a pickup and return date. The admin will approve your reservation based on availability.
Go to the "Report Dashboard" in your student dashboard and file an incident report (LF-05). Describe the issue, select the affected item, and submit. Admins will review and take appropriate action.

About LabLinx

LabLinx DLSU-D is a web-based resource management system developed to replace manual tracking methods at the De La Salle University-Dasmariñas High School Complex. It streamlines the monitoring, borrowing, and reservation of lab equipment, robotics equipment, sports equipment, and facilities. With real-time tracking and automated notifications, LabLinx enhances accountability and efficiency in resource usage.
